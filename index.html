Why We Analyzed Mess Attendance?

Every day, thousands of students rely on college mess facilities for meals. Yet, fluctuating attendance leads to food wastage and overcrowding. This analysis aims to uncover patterns in student dining habits to optimize food planning and improve the mess experience.

Understanding Student Engagement with Mess Facilities

Efficient mess management is crucial for ensuring smooth dining experiences and reducing food waste. This analysis aims to identify peak meal times and meal-skipping trends across different study years, helping mess staff optimize food preparation schedules.

How We Collected the Data

The dataset was gathered through Google Forms, where students self-reported their mess attendance habits. The survey included multiple-choice and time-based questions on meal timings, study year, and perceived crowd levels.
